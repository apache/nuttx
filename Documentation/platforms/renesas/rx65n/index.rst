=============
Renesas RX65N
=============

Support for the Renesas RX65N family was released in NuttX with a
contribution from Anjana. Two boards are supported in this initial
release:

-  **RSK RX65N-2MB**.
-  **GR-Rose**.

**STATUS**

-  **NuttX-8.2**
-  **NuttX-9.0** RTC driver for the RX65N was added.
